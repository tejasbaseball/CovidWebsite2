How did the Coronavirus spread?

  • December 8th A patient in the city of Wuhan sought medical help for pneumonia-like symptoms.
  • December 29th Local hospitals in Hubei report the first four cases of a “pneumonia of unknown etiology.”
  • January 1st The seafood market (Huanan Seafood Wholesale Market) that was identified as a suspected center of the outbreak is closed.
  • January 3rd China officially notifies the WHO of an outbreak.
  • January 13th Thailand reports its first case, a sixty-one year old woman who did not visit the seafood market.
  • January 14th Wuhan implements screening measures for travelers leaving the city at airports, railway stations, and other passenger terminals.
  • February 1st First Cases Appear in United States and Europe
  • February 3rd First Cases Appear in Many More Countries; Travel Bans Spread
